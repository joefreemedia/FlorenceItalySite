San Marco

Michelangelo's David

The galleries of Florence's Art Academy hold Michelangelo's David and unfinished Prisoner sculptures, as well as numerous other masterpieces. Visitors can also tour the nearby church and monastery of San Marco, filled with works by Renaissance master Fra Angelico, including the monks' sparely decorated cells.
