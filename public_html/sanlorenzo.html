San Lorenzo

Markets and Medici

The Central Market inside the building in the San Lorenzo neighborhood offers stalls selling numerous fresh food ingredients. Along the streets outside is a separate market selling leather goods, paper and other souvenir items. Directly across from the markets is the Basilica of San Lorenzo. Its unfinished exterior belies the artistic gems to be found inside including the Laurentian Library, one of Michelangelo's architectural masterpieces. Also part of the San Lorenzo complex are the Medici Chapels, which include Michelangelo's magnificent tomb sculptures, Dawn and Dusk, Night and Day. Down the street is the Medici's Palace, built for the banking family between 1444 and 1484. One of the highlights is the Chapel of the Magi frescoed by Benozzo Gozzoli.
